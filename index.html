Side Tipper Trans-38 TC

Trans Side Tipper-38 is an accessory designed to facilitate the transport and side unloading of agricultural materials and other products. This tipper has a considerable capacity, allowing players to handle large volumes of cargo efficiently. The side unloading feature is a key aspect that offers flexibility in material handling, especially in tight spaces where rear unloading might not be feasible.
